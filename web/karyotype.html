Meiotic segregation — 6 possible gamete types
Robertsonian carriers are phenotypically normal but produce unbalanced gametes. Empirical risk of liveborn trisomy is much lower than theoretical (most unbalanced conceptions miscarry early). For rob(13;14): ~1% risk of Patau in offspring.
